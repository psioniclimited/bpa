ABOUT US

In 2009, BLUE PLANET Group had a thought of moving to textile industries since they are already producing high quality yarn. However the challenge has to have, a world class textile, meeting all compliances, produces high end knitwear. By this, they created a team of experts from leading textile industries in Bangladesh. The management team has a total of over 40 years in the industry. BPKW have a composite garment manufacturing facility comprising of spinning (Badar spinning Mills Ltd.), Knitting, Printing, Embroidery and Packing in a single place. This enables us to provide fast and timely delivery of orders.
